# ASSET Pre-Test 題庫讀書筆記

## 1. Which edition of ASSET you learn now?

選項：

- first edition
- second edition
- third edition
- 4th edition

考點：Pre-Test 答案 key 與 Wistia 課程影片標題可能不一致。

解題邏輯：正確答案為 4th edition，已通過表單驗證。Wistia 頻道標題雖為「ASSET - 2nd Ed」，但 Pre-Test 答案 key 以 4th edition 為準。

錯誤選項辨識：不要以線上影片標題或個人印象推測版本，應以表單驗證的答案 key 為最後依據。

回看資料：ASSET 4th edition 課程頁面。

## 2. The purpose of the Cattell-Braasch maneuver in combination with the Kocher maneuver is to

選項：

- Mobilize the small bowel, duodenum, head of the pancreas, stomach, and kidney
- Mobilize the descending colon, spleen, tail of the pancreas, stomach, and kidney
- Visualize the abdominal aorta, celiac trunk, SMA, IMA, and systemic veins
- Visualize the retroperitoneal structures on the right mid abdomen and pelvis

考點：右側 medial visceral rotation 與 Kocher maneuver 的暴露範圍。

解題邏輯：Cattell-Braasch 是右側內臟旋轉，Kocher maneuver 處理 duodenum/head of pancreas。合併使用時重點是進入右側 retroperitoneum，特別是右中腹到骨盆的 retroperitoneal structures。

錯誤選項辨識：提到 descending colon、spleen、tail of pancreas 多半偏向左側 medial visceral rotation。若選項把 celiac trunk、SMA、IMA 全部列為主目標，要注意是否混入 Mattox/left medial visceral rotation 或 central vascular exposure 的概念。

回看資料：腹部與骨盆 exposure 筆記；Pre-Test study guide 的 Abdomen / Retroperitoneum / Pelvis。

## 3. A 19-year-old male presents to the ED status post GSW to the right thigh. ABI left 1.0, right 0.8. What is the MOST appropriate next step?

選項：

- Evaluate the extremity using Doppler ultrasound
- Take him to the operating room for on table angiogram
- Order a CT angiogram of the extremity
- Observe him in the hospital with serial measurement of ABI

考點：穿刺性肢體外傷、ABI abnormal、hard signs vs soft signs。

解題邏輯：ABI 低於正常但題幹沒有描述 active hemorrhage、expanding hematoma、ischemia 等 hard signs，通常進入 vascular imaging 決策。穩定病人以影像定位血管傷較合理。

錯誤選項辨識：直接進 OR 適合 hard signs 或不穩定。單純 observation 較適合 ABI 正常且無 hard signs。Doppler 可作 bedside 評估，但若 ABI 已 abnormal，需思考是否需要更完整的 vascular map。

回看影片：Femoral exposure、Popliteal exposure。

## 4. What is the most reliable palpable EXTERNAL landmark enabling orientation of the anatomy when performing a cricothyroidotomy?

選項：

- The cricoid cartilage
- The thyroid cartilage
- The cricothyroid membrane
- The sternal notch

考點：外部可觸診 landmark 與實際切開位置的差異。

解題邏輯：題目問 palpable external landmark，不是問最後切開的膜。臨床上需先摸到可靠、較大的硬骨性/軟骨性標誌，再定位 cricothyroid membrane。

錯誤選項辨識：cricothyroid membrane 是目標切開位置，但腫脹、肥胖、血腫時不一定是最可靠外部定位點。sternal notch 太低，不是 cricothyroidotomy 的直接定位核心。

回看資料：Airway / Neck 筆記。

## 5. Which approach is the MOST appropriate as the initial incision for an unstable patient with thoracic trauma and suspected cardiac injury?

選項：

- Trap door thoracotomy
- Right anterolateral thoracotomy
- Left anterolateral thoracotomy
- Clamshell bilateral thoracotomy

考點：不穩定胸部創傷、疑似 cardiac injury 的初始切口。

解題邏輯：先想最快解除 tamponade、進行 open cardiac massage、控制 descending thoracic aorta 的入口。初始切口與後續可延伸切口要分開。

錯誤選項辨識：clamshell 是可由左前外側胸廓切開延伸而來的廣泛暴露，但題目問 initial incision。right-sided approach 需有特定右胸來源；trap door 不是常規初始 resuscitative incision。

回看影片：ED/Resuscitative thoracotomy、Clamshell thoracotomy。

## 6. The most reliable early finding of compartment syndrome of the lower leg in a patient who can participate in an exam is:

選項：

- Pulselessness in the injured limb
- Paresthesia between the toes of the affected limb
- Pain greater than expected due to the injury
- The affected limb feels tense or hard

考點：compartment syndrome 早期臨床表現。

解題邏輯：能配合檢查的病人，早期最重要的是疼痛與被動牽拉痛；pulselessness 是晚期。題目強調 early finding 與 can participate in exam。

錯誤選項辨識：pulselessness 代表太晚。paresthesia 與 tense compartment 重要但不是最可靠早期表現。

回看影片：Fasciotomy。

## 7. After exposure of the brachial artery in the distal arm, what is the BEST way to identify the brachial bifurcation and proximal radial and ulnar arteries?

選項：

- Extending the incision obliquely across the antecubital fossa
- Division of the brachioradialis muscle in the proximal forearm
- Dissecting to expose the median nerve
- Division of the bicipital aponeurosis

考點：brachial artery bifurcation 的 gateway。

解題邏輯：brachial exposure 影片強調 bicipital aponeurosis 覆蓋 bicipital tendon，是進入 bifurcation、radial/ulnar proximal vessels 的關鍵層。

錯誤選項辨識：median nerve 是重要 landmark，但找到 nerve 不等於打開 bifurcation。brachioradialis 更偏 distal forearm/radial artery exposure。延長切口可能需要，但不是辨識 bifurcation 的關鍵 maneuver。

回看影片：Brachial exposure。

## 8. Which statement is true regarding Zone 2 of the neck?

選項：

- It is the smallest region of the neck, bounded by the bony structures of the sternum and mandible
- In the hemodynamically abnormal patient, injuries require mandatory surgical intervention
- Injuries in the zone are optimally exposed via clavicle resection or median sternotomy
- In the hemodynamically normal patient, injuries are best examined using ultrasound

考點：neck zone、hemodynamic status、hard signs。

解題邏輯：Zone 2 penetrating neck trauma 的處理首先看生理狀態。Hemodynamically abnormal 或 hard signs 時，不應延遲 definitive control。

錯誤選項辨識：clavicle resection/median sternotomy 偏 subclavian/thoracic inlet 或 chest great vessel exposure，不是 Zone 2 的標準最佳暴露。穩定病人常以 CTA/選擇性處理，不是以 ultrasound 作主要最佳檢查。

回看影片：Carotid exposure、Subclavian exposure above clavicle。

## 9. Where is the MOST appropriate skin incision for exposure of the common femoral artery located?

選項：

- Extends along the crease between the abdominal skin and the upper thigh
- Extends across the inguinal ligament onto the upper thigh approximately 2 fingerbreadths lateral to the ASIS
- Extends from just above the inguinal ligament along medial border of sartorius muscle onto the upper thigh
- Extends curvilinearly from 2cm above ASIS to just above inguinal ligament

考點：common femoral artery exposure 的 skin incision。

解題邏輯：影片強調 vertical incision，從 inguinal ligament 上方往 upper thigh 延伸，沿 sartorius medial margin，直接對準 femoral pulse 或其解剖定位。

錯誤選項辨識：inguinal crease 橫切不利於 vascular exposure 延伸。ASIS lateral 或 curvilinear iliac incision 比較像 iliac/retroperitoneal exposure，不是 common femoral artery 的主要切口。

回看影片：Femoral exposure。

## 10. Stab wound to right thigh with active bleeding and enlarging hematoma despite tourniquet. Once in OR, preferred area for sterile prepping?

選項：

- The right leg circumferentially from groin to toes, and the left groin to above-knee for potential vein harvest and/or angiogram
- The right leg circumferentially from groin to knee, and the left groin to above knee for potential vein harvest and/or angiogram
- The abdomen and both legs circumferentially from groin to toes
- The right leg circumferentially from groin to toes

考點：vascular trauma OR preparation。

解題邏輯：需要準備受傷肢體完整長度，以便 proximal/distal control、fasciotomy 或 extension；也要準備對側 groin/thigh 作 potential vein harvest 或 access。

錯誤選項辨識：只準備到 knee 可能限制 distal exposure/fasciotomy。只準備患側忽略 conduit。準備 abdomen 與雙腿全長可能過度，除非題幹暗示腹腔或雙側需求。

回看影片：Femoral exposure、Popliteal exposure、Fasciotomy。

## 11. Which of the following is required for successful management of anterior tracheal injuries?

選項：

- Debridement of injury and primary repair
- Using strap muscles to buttress repair
- Partial median sternotomy to expose the injury
- Protection of the repair with a tracheostomy

考點：anterior tracheal injury 的基本處理原則。

解題邏輯：先處理受損組織並完成 primary repair。Buttress、sternotomy、tracheostomy 是特定情境下的 adjunct，不是所有 anterior injury 的必要條件。

錯誤選項辨識：看到 required 要排除「有時需要」但非必要的動作。Sternotomy 只在遠端或胸入口特殊暴露時考慮。

回看資料：Neck / Airway 筆記。

## 12. What structure is commonly divided to expose the origin of the vertebral artery?

選項：

- Anterior scalene muscle
- Cricopharyngeal muscle
- Omohyoid muscle
- Sternocleidomastoid muscle

考點：vertebral artery origin 與 subclavian exposure。

解題邏輯：vertebral artery origin 來自 subclavian artery，暴露此區常涉及 supraclavicular approach、保護 phrenic nerve，並處理 anterior scalene。

錯誤選項辨識：omohyoid 常在 carotid exposure 中處理。cricopharyngeal 偏 esophagus/pharynx。SCM head 可能在 subclavian exposure 中切開，但 vertebral origin 的核心 deeper landmark 是 anterior scalene。

回看影片：Subclavian exposure above clavicle。

## 13. Unstable patient with injury and occlusion of posterior tibial artery sparing anterior tibial and peroneal arteries. Most appropriate management?

選項：

- Endovascular stenting to minimize ischemia of the posteromedial foot
- Non operative management with close monitoring of distal perfusion
- Operative exposure and shunting of the vessel, with formal repair done after the patient has been stabilized
- Operative expose and ligation of the posterior tibial artery, as the other two vessels will ensure that the foot is well perfused.

考點：unstable damage-control 情境下單支 tibial vessel 的處理原則。

解題邏輯：病人不穩定，damage-control 優先處理危及生命的出血來源。anterior tibial 與 peroneal artery intact 時，posterior tibial 單支 occluded 對 distal perfusion 威脅有限，不需為了 shunting 延長手術時間。

錯誤選項辨識：stent 不適合 unstable 病人。shunt 主要用於多條 runoff 受損或 threatened limb。ligation 在 damage-control 可接受但 non-operative 為 best answer。

回看影片：Popliteal exposure、Fasciotomy。

## 14. Useful modalities to control bleeding from a pelvic fracture include all EXCEPT:

選項：

- Pelvic binder
- Intra-abdominal packing
- Selective embolization of internal iliac arteries
- REBOA

考點：pelvic fracture hemorrhage control 的解剖空間。

解題邏輯：pelvic fracture 出血多在 retroperitoneal/preperitoneal space；有效工具包括 binder、preperitoneal pelvic packing、angiographic embolization、REBOA bridge。

錯誤選項辨識：intra-abdominal packing 是 peritoneal cavity 的 packing，不能等同 pelvic preperitoneal packing。

回看影片：Pelvic packing。

## 15. Which is NOT an absolute indication for operative intervention for renal trauma?

選項：

- Hemodynamically stable patient with vascular blush on CT
- Hemodynamically stable patient with expanding hematoma in Gerota's fascia
- Hemodynamically unstable patient with positive FAST exam
- Hemodynamically stable patient with active bleeding into the peritoneal cavity from the kidney

考點：renal trauma operative indication vs nonoperative/angioembolization。

解題邏輯：stable patient with contrast blush often enters selective nonoperative management or angioembolization pathway。Unstable physiology 或 intraperitoneal active bleeding 更偏 operative indication。

錯誤選項辨識：題目問 NOT absolute indication，需找「可用非手術或 IR 管理」的穩定情境。

回看資料：Abdomen / Retroperitoneum 筆記。

## 16. Gunshot pelvis with 50% transection of left common iliac artery; ongoing bleeding despite clamping proximal left common iliac and proximal left external iliac artery. Next step?

選項：

- Clamp the left common iliac vein
- Clamp the left internal iliac artery
- Clamp the aorta just above its bifurcation
- Pack the pelvis and proceed to angiography

考點：iliac artery branch control 與 persistent pelvic bleeding。

解題邏輯：若已 clamp common iliac proximal 與 external iliac，仍 bleeding，需思考 remaining inflow/outflow 或 branch source，尤其 internal iliac contribution。

錯誤選項辨識：clamp vein 不會解決 arterial inflow。直接 pack/angiography 不適合已在 OR 且有可疑 uncontrolled arterial source。Aortic clamp 是更 proximal escalation，但先判斷是否有未控制的 branch。

回看影片：Iliac exposure、Pelvic packing。

## 17. Hemodynamically normal stab wound left of sternum with equivocal cardiac FAST. Most appropriate operative intervention?

選項：

- Median Sternotomy
- Pericardial Window
- Right Anterolateral Thoracotomy
- Left Anterolateral Thoracotomy

考點：stable suspected cardiac injury 的診斷性介入。

解題邏輯：hemodynamically normal 且 FAST equivocal，重點是確認 pericardial blood，而不是直接最大暴露。Pericardial window 是常見 diagnostic/therapeutic bridge。

錯誤選項辨識：sternotomy 或 thoracotomy 適合 confirmed injury 或 unstable physiology。right anterolateral 與題幹 left parasternal 不匹配。

回看影片：Median sternotomy、ED/Resuscitative thoracotomy。

## 18. Trans-mediastinal gunshot wound: exposure of distal third of esophagus best accomplished with which incision?

選項：

- Left Posterolateral Thoracotomy
- Midline Laparotomy
- Left Anterolateral Thoracotomy
- Right Anterolateral Thoracotomy

考點：esophagus segment 與 thoracic approach。

解題邏輯：distal third esophagus 位於 lower thorax/near hiatus，通常以 left thoracic route 取得較佳 exposure。需分清 cervical、upper thoracic、distal thoracic esophagus。

錯誤選項辨識：right thoracic 更常用於 upper/mid thoracic esophagus。midline laparotomy 對 intra-abdominal distal esophagus/GE junction 有用，但題目問 transmediastinal distal third exposure 要注意胸腔段。

回看資料：Chest / Esophagus exposure 筆記。

## 19. Optimal operative exposure for unstable trauma patient with injury to anterior medial portion of lung?

選項：

- Median sternotomy
- Posterolateral thoracotomy
- Anterolateral thoracotomy
- Trap door incision

考點：unstable lung injury 的快速 thoracic exposure。

解題邏輯：unstable trauma 先選快速可進入胸腔、可控制 anterior/medial lung 或 hilum 的 route。Posterolateral exposure 較完整但不適合急救初始速度。

錯誤選項辨識：median sternotomy 偏心臟與 anterior mediastinum。trap door 是更特殊 exposure，不是常規 unstable chest trauma initial approach。

回看影片：ED/Resuscitative thoracotomy、Clamshell thoracotomy。

## 20. Arrest 2 minutes after GSW to right chest. Most appropriate initial step to obtain hemorrhage control?

選項：

- Median sternotomy
- Left anterolateral thoracotomy
- Right anterolateral thoracotomy
- Placing a REBOA catheter in Zone I

考點：traumatic arrest with suspected right chest hemorrhage。

解題邏輯：題幹指出 right chest penetrating injury 且 arrest，hemorrhage control 需進入受傷胸腔。要分清 cardiac tamponade/aortic cross-clamp 的 left ED thoracotomy 與 right chest source control。

錯誤選項辨識：REBOA 不控制胸腔出血。Median sternotomy 對 right lateral chest source 不一定最快。Left thoracotomy雖是 resuscitative classic，但若題目明確右胸出血，需考慮右側控制。

回看影片：ED/Resuscitative thoracotomy、Clamshell thoracotomy。

## 21. Shot in left upper chest, barely palpable BP, pulsatile bleeding beneath clavicle. Optimal surgical exposure?

選項：

- Anterolateral thoracotomy
- Third intercostal space thoracotomy
- Resection of the medial third of the clavicle
- Clamshell thoracotomy

考點：left subclavian / thoracic outlet vascular injury exposure。

解題邏輯：pulsatile bleeding beneath clavicle 指向 subclavian vessel region。要選能直接暴露 thoracic outlet/subclavian 的 approach，必要時需要 clavicle 部分切除。

錯誤選項辨識：anterolateral 或 clamshell 可進胸，但 subclavian 尤其 clavicle 下方仍可能 posterior/deep，未必提供最佳 direct exposure。

回看影片：Subclavian exposure above clavicle、Clamshell thoracotomy。

## 22. MVC, positive FAST, hypotensive despite blood; laparotomy shows high-grade liver laceration with active bleeding. Most appropriate initial step?

選項：

- Packing within the peri-hepatic spaces and liver parenchyma
- Bimanual compression the liver back to its normal shape.
- Implement the Pringle maneuver
- Apply aortic cross clamp to control liver hemorrhage

考點：liver damage-control sequence。

解題邏輯：高級肝裂傷大量出血，初始是快速 damage control：壓迫、packing、恢復肝形狀並暫時止血。Pringle 是判斷 inflow bleeding 的 maneuver，不應跳過最直接的 hemorrhage control。

錯誤選項辨識：aortic cross-clamp 不是 liver-specific hemorrhage 的首選。Pringle 對 hepatic venous/retrohepatic bleeding 不夠。題目若問 initial step，優先想 immediate packing/compression。

回看資料：Abdomen / Liver trauma 筆記。

## 23. The Cattell-Braasch maneuver exposes which of the following?

選項：

- Celiac trunk
- Descending thoracic aorta
- Left renal artery
- Inferior vena cava

考點：右側 medial visceral rotation 暴露結構。

解題邏輯：Cattell-Braasch 是右側 approach，重點在右側 retroperitoneum 與 IVC 相關 exposure。

錯誤選項辨識：descending thoracic aorta 是胸腔。left renal artery 與左側 rotation/central exposure 更相關。celiac trunk 更偏 supraceliac/upper abdominal central exposure。

回看資料：Abdomen / Retroperitoneum 筆記。

## 24. Trauma laparotomy in hypotensive patient with large Zone 1 hematoma. Best way to obtain initial proximal aortic control?

選項：

- Perform a resuscitative thoracotomy and cross-clamp the aorta
- Place a REBOA catheter into Zone I of the aorta
- Divide the left crus of the diaphragm to expose and clamp the aorta
- Manually compress the aorta against the vertebral bodies

考點：已開腹狀態下的 immediate aortic control。

解題邏輯：已經在 laparotomy 內且面對 Zone 1 hematoma，最快 initial control 是手壓 aorta against spine。之後才進一步 supraceliac exposure/clamp。

錯誤選項辨識：thoracotomy 與 REBOA 是 resuscitation choices，但已開腹時不是最快 initial step。divide crus 是取得正式 clamp 的步驟，但不是最立即的初始控制。

回看資料：Abdomen / Retroperitoneum 筆記。

## 25. Open-book pelvis fracture, binder and blood no response, no other hemorrhage source. Most appropriate temporizing measure?

選項：

- REBOA
- External fixation of the pelvic ring
- Laparotomy with ligation of internal iliac arteries
- Initiate massive transfusion protocol

考點：unstable pelvic hemorrhage temporization。

解題邏輯：已 binder 且仍 shock，且已辨識 pelvis 為出血來源。Temporizing measure 需快速橋接 definitive pelvic hemorrhage control。

錯誤選項辨識：MTP 是 resuscitation 必要背景，但題目問 hemorrhage temporizing measure。Internal iliac ligation 不常作為首選。External fixation 有穩定作用，但 profound shock 時需考慮更快速 central control bridge。

回看影片：Pelvic packing。

## 26. Recommended time limit of occlusion after successful Aortic Zone I balloon occlusion catheter?

選項：

- < 30 minutes
- < 45 minutes
- < 60 minutes
- < 90 minutes

考點：Zone I REBOA occlusion time safety。

解題邏輯：Zone I occlusion ischemic burden 高，作答以最保守安全上限為優先。重點是「短時間橋接」，不是 definitive control。

錯誤選項辨識：時間越長，visceral/spinal/lower body ischemia 越高；長時間選項通常不符合 current safety framing。

回看資料：REBOA / Pelvic hemorrhage 筆記。

## 27. Stab to right anterior neck at thyroid cartilage level, pulsatile bleeding and hypotension. Most appropriate management?

選項：

- Operative exploration via a transverse collar incision
- Operative exploration via right anterior sternocleidomastoid incision
- Endovascular evaluation and stenting
- CT angiography to identify the site of bleeding

考點：unstable Zone 2 penetrating neck injury。

解題邏輯：pulsatile bleeding + hypotension 是 hard signs/hemodynamic abnormal。需 immediate operative exploration。切口需配合 right anterior neck vascular exposure。

錯誤選項辨識：CTA 與 endovascular 適合更穩定或特定 vascular lesion。Transverse collar incision 偏 thyroid/trachea/cervical exploration，不是 carotid sheath vascular exposure 的標準路徑。

回看影片：Carotid exposure。

## 28. What is the optimal incision to expose the distal tracheal?

選項：

- Midline over the larynx and trachea
- Anterior border of the left sternocleidomastoid
- Transverse 1-2 cm above the manubrium
- Limited median sternotomy

考點：distal cervical trachea 至 thoracic inlet 的 optimal incision。

解題邏輯：最佳初始 incision 為 midline over larynx and trachea，提供直接 midline access 至 distal trachea，並保留向上或向下（含 sternotomy）延伸的彈性。

錯誤選項辨識：limited sternotomy 是延伸選項而非初始 optimal incision。transverse 1-2 cm above manubrium 雖可達低位 trachea，但不是標準 midline tracheal exposure 起手式。SCM anterior border 是 lateral neck 路線。

回看資料：Neck / Airway 筆記。

## 29. Exploring left neck Zone 2 GSW. Which structure overlies the bifurcation of the common carotid artery?

選項：

- Omohyoid muscle
- Hypoglossal nerve
- Common facial vein
- Digastric muscle

考點：carotid bifurcation exposure anatomy。

解題邏輯：carotid exposure 時需辨識並處理影響 bifurcation 暴露的 crossing venous structure。Hypoglossal nerve 在上方，是要保護的 landmark。

錯誤選項辨識：omohyoid 較低位且常需 divided 以增加 exposure。digastric 偏上頸部。hypoglossal 重要但不是典型 overlying bifurcation 的 crossing vein。

回看影片：Carotid exposure。

## 30. Ideal positioning for trauma patient with injury to popliteal artery?

選項：

- Supine with hip abduction, slight lateral rotation of the leg, and arms tucked
- Supine with hip adduction, slight medial rotation of the leg, and arms out
- Supine with hip adduction, slight medial rotation of the leg, and a bump under the thighs
- Supine with hip abduction, slight lateral rotation of the leg, and arms out

考點：popliteal artery medial approach positioning。

解題邏輯：popliteal exposure 常從 medial side 取得 above/below knee access，因此需讓 medial thigh/leg 可接近，同時保留麻醉與血管 access。

錯誤選項辨識：adduction/medial rotation 會限制 medial exposure。arms tucked 可能限制 IV/monitoring/access；題目若強調 trauma patient，需思考整體 access。

回看影片：Popliteal exposure。

## 31. Which statement is TRUE regarding the Pringle maneuver?

選項：

- It is effective in controlling bleeding from hepatic artery injury
- It is effective in controlling bleeding from the hepatic vein
- It is effective when a patient has a replaced left hepatic artery
- It can be safely used for up to 45 minutes while repairing hepatic injuries

考點：Pringle maneuver 控制範圍與限制。

解題邏輯：Pringle occludes portal triad inflow，因此控制 hepatic artery/portal vein inflow bleeding。它不控制 hepatic venous outflow 或 retrohepatic IVC bleeding。

錯誤選項辨識：hepatic vein bleeding 不會被 Pringle 完全控制。Replaced arterial anatomy 可能繞過 porta hepatis control。安全時間不是無限制，且 trauma physiology 下不宜用長時間作答。

回看資料：Liver hemorrhage 筆記。

## 32. What anatomical structure is in retroperitoneal Zone 2?

選項：

- Pancreas
- Ureter
- Bladder
- Duodenum

考點：retroperitoneal zone anatomy。

解題邏輯：Zone 2 是 lateral/perirenal retroperitoneum，常包含 kidney、renal vessels、ureter 等結構。

錯誤選項辨識：pancreas/duodenum 偏 central upper retroperitoneal/Zone 1 相關。bladder 更偏 pelvic/Zone 3。

回看資料：Retroperitoneal zones 筆記。

## 33. Division of which can assist in improved exposure of the subclavian artery?

選項：

- Pectoralis major muscle
- Omohyoid muscle
- Body of the clavicle
- Posterior scalene muscle

考點：subclavian artery exposure escalation。

解題邏輯：subclavian artery 因 clavicle 遮蔽，必要時需要切除 clavicle 的一部分來改善 exposure，尤其 trauma bleeding beneath clavicle 的情境。

錯誤選項辨識：pectoralis major 與 axillary exposure 較相關。omohyoid 偏 carotid exposure。posterior scalene 不是標準 improve subclavian exposure 的主要結構；supraclavicular exposure 常處理 anterior scalene。

回看影片：Subclavian exposure above clavicle、Clamshell thoracotomy。
